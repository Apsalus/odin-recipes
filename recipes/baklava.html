How to make baklava

What is baklava

Baklava comes in many varieties but is essentially a layered pastry with nut filling soaked in sweet syrup. This is a shameless reproduction of Chef John's baklava, though I do modify the recipe very slightly.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • extra virgin olive oil (evoo)
  • white wine vinegar

Steps

  1. Make a craggy dough
  2. Split into 20g balls
  3. Roll each ball super flat
  4. Layer together with butter and nuts
