Spanish-style Lentil Soup

What is this?

This soup (stew?) is one of my all-time favorite recipes in the world. I could probably eat the result of this recipe just about every day. It's a lentil soup recipe from Spain that my grandmother used to make for me, and, while objectively peasant food, it is positively divine.

Ingredients

  • lentils
  • extra virgin olive oil (evoo)
  • potatoes
  • chorizo
  • garlic

Steps

  1. Boil lentils
  2. Quarter some potatoes
  3. Bite sized chorizo chunks into mix
  4. Cook for 4 hours
